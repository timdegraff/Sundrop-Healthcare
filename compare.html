What the extra premium buys

The two premiums are close, but the coverage isn't. G15 charges 20% coinsurance after its deductible, so a hospital birth, ER trip, or surgery can still cost the member thousands. G121 charges 0% coinsurance after a low (~$1,200) deductible, so even a big medical year stays near that deductible. In any year with real hospital use, G121's slightly higher premium is almost always cheaper all-in.
