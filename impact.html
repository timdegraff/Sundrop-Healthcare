How it's computed: Sundrop's share = the Free Basic (HSA POS Bronze B80) premium at the employee's age — paid for both plans. Gold-pickers pay the difference up to the HMO Gold G121 premium. Rates are the 8/1/2026 age-banded premiums (incl. taxes/fees), employee-only. Dependents (spouse/kids) are the employee's cost and aren't shown here.

One decision to make

Because premiums are age-rated, the Gold buy-up an employee pays rises with age (~$136/mo at 23 → ~$169 at 37 → ~$304 at 55). This model has Sundrop pay 100% of Basic at each age, so Sundrop's cost stays predictable and the employee covers the gap. Alternatively Sundrop could charge a flat Gold price for everyone and absorb the age difference — fairer-feeling for staff, but Sundrop takes on more for older employees.
